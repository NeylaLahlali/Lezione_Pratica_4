Karlotta Freier

is an accomplished Brooklyn-based artist with a background in illustration. In 2021, she graduated
from the MFA Illustration as Visual Essay program at the School of Visual Arts. In addition to her
creative pursuits, Karlotta also dedicates her time to sharing her knowledge with the next
generation of artists.
She has taught illustration at the China Academy of Art and spoken as a guest speaker at
universities across the United States.
